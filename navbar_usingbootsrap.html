EXPLORE THE HYDERABAD

RAMOJI FILM CITY

Experience the magic of cinema and Bollywood at Ramoji Film City, a spectacular getaway outside Hyderabad. Enjoy studio tours, adventure sports, live shows, thematic attractions, gardens and more in this Guinness World Record holder.

CHARMINAR

A visit to the Charminar in Hyderabad is as compulsive as a visit to the Eiffel tower in Paris. Constructed in 1591 by Mohammed Quli Qutub Shah of Shah Dynasty, Charminar, means four turrets (char- four, minar-turret) in Urdu.

GOLKONDA

The Sound and Light Show at Golconda Fort vividly narrates the fort's rich history, bringing to life the tales of its grandeur and legendary rulers. Held in the fort's lush open-air surroundings, the show uses dramatic lighting and captivating storytelling, creating an immersive experience for visitors.
